Yadav Stones

Established in 2001, Yadav Stones has grown to become a premier manufacturer of construction materials in Hubli, Karnataka. Located strategically on Neeligin Road near Channama Circle, we specialize in high-quality crushed stone, cement blocks, and stone base solutions for both local and regional clients.

With over two decades of industry experience, we've built a reputation for reliability and quality. Our 7+ acre mining facility operates with full regulatory compliance, holding all necessary licenses including LE-3 and LE-10 magazine licenses for explosive material storage, along with approvals from DGMS (Directorate General of Mining Safety) and KSPCB (Karnataka State Pollution Control Board).
